This is my second project from "Das Wotan-Projekt"! Well, as few of you know, I'm a newly beginner web-dev programmer, a long-shot lawyer and a old-timer pharmacist... Yeah, I know what you are think, how can someone like Ragnarök On-line, Final Fantasy Tactics (best game ever) and Captain Tsubasa J?

I'll explain: my life is a mess... I wanted to be a doctor, ended-up graduating on Pharmacy, than migrated to court rooms (1 time as criminal - minor offense, 100's other times as a solo lawyer) and than found myself on the edges of the internet, watching this great web behind it's curtains, and learning to program...

By the time I'm reaching my 35's, I realized that programming, developing programs and constructing web-sites IS MY THING! I finally found something that makes me whole! But, isn't that a bit too late? Short answer: yes...

I got bills to pay, a house to keep and work late hours so I can keep my dream of becoming a full-stack web-developer alive... But, you know what? It's worth it! After all, I'll never stop studying and learning new things! And with time, my chances of making the wrong choices lower as I get wiser and... older, ok, yeah.

Anyway, here is another project, hope you guys enjoy and please send me hello on any link to any of my pages you may find in here! o/
